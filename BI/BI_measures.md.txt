# BI Measures

### Implemented Measures
1. **Order Number Distinct Count**  
   - Counts unique order numbers across the dataset.

2. **Cancel Percentage**  
   - Formula:  
     \[
     \text{Cancel %} = \frac{\text{Orders with StatusCode = 3}}{\text{Total Orders}}
     \]
