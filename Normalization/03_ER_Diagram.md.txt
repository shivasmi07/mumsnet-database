# ER Diagram

The final schema after applying 3NF resulted in 9 tables, connected as follows:

- **CustomerCity** → linked to **Customer**  
- **Product** → linked to **Variant** and **VariantProductGroup**  
- **Order** → linked to **OrderItem** and **Customer**  

Diagram:  
![ER Diagram](imgages/er-diagram.png)

This structure removes redundancy and provides a strong foundation for BI and reporting.
